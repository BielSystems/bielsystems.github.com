SEEKING A PERFECT PARTNER: Why HE:labs?

Atende Simples has a very complex operation, that involves a lot of technologies, companies and systems. The voice platform was the responsibility of VoxAge in São Paulo. It was necessary to decide the best way to create our web application:

  • Hire developers
  • Hire freelancers
  • Hire a development company

The first two options had various risks:

  • Would need someone to control the work of developers, putting weight on the structure early on our company.
  • There was a possibility of creating a dependency of freelancers on a heated labor market.
  • If a developer or freelancer leaves the project, the impact to get another one and put everything back on track would certainly be considerable.

Telephony Middleware (?)

For some tasks, the team had to study specifics of the application domain. O telephony back-end is a example. We were building a telephonic middleware in the cloud, and behind, the system had to be composed in large 3 blocks:

  • A web interface, for the final users, where everithing must be simple. Here is where all end users of the service are related.
  • A midfield acting as the glue between the settings that the end user does, and telephony services. This glue is a web service that runs on the same physical infrastructure of telephony, for performance reasons.
  • The phone system itself, which is developed by VoxAge.

A flow of buying simply magical

Atende Simples is being used by companies of different sizes and in all areas. It has sophisticated features such as call recording, control of operating hours of the service, call blocking, reports... all with great simplicity! Hiring is done in 6 steps:

  1. Choose a business phone: 0800 or national number
  2. Register your email and password
  3. Sign phones to receive calls
  4. Choose the type of service, with menu or automatic
  5. Enter your welcome message or an audio file
  6. Choose what will happen with calls
